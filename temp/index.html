Telemedicine enables remote diagnosis and treatment of patients through video conferencing and digital communication. It allows healthcare providers to reach patients in remote areas, offer virtual consultations, monitor chronic conditions remotely, and provide timely medical.

Robotic Surgery

Robotic surgery involves using robotic systems to assist surgeons in performing complex procedures with precision, flexibility, and minimally invasive techniques. These systems provide enhanced visualization, dexterity, and control, resulting in smaller incisions, reduced pain, shorter recovery times.
